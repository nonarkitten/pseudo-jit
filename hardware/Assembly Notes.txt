Assembly Notes

- All SMT components are on the TOP
- Thru-hole headers are to be soldered on the BOTTOM
- Board is intended to be install like a DIP chip
- Board is quite dense; suggest installing small components first
- PCB Copper Layer Orders (numbers are out-of-order)
	* TopLayer
	* Inner1
	* Inner2
	* Inner3
	* Inner4
	* BottomLayer
